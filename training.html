Training Session

In addition to the lectures given by the invited speakers, a half-day training session on a time-dependent ab initio non-adiabatic molecular dynamics package (Hefei-NAMD) will be delivered. Hefei-NAMD is an ab initio nonadiabatic molecular dynamics program to investigate the ultrafast excited carrier dynamics in real and momentum space, energy and time scale.

The ppt for the training session are now available for download.
